## 1) 60s Master Ad — "Command the Work"

### Positioning
Route5 is not reminders software. It is an execution operating system:
**Signal -> Structure -> Assign -> Execute -> Visibility**

### Audience
Investors, founders, operators, and managers evaluating execution tooling.

### Runtime
60 seconds (16:9 master + 9:16 social cut)

### Scene Timeline

#### Scene 1 — Chaos (0:00-0:07)
- Visual: Slack pings, email chain overload, sticky notes, confused team.
- Text: "Work is everywhere. Ownership is nowhere."
- VO: "Every team has the same problem: decisions happen fast... execution gets lost."

#### Scene 2 — The Shift (0:07-0:13)
- Visual: Route5 login -> dashboard loads smoothly.
- Text: "Route5"
- VO: "Route5 turns scattered signals into accountable execution."

#### Scene 3 — Capture Intelligence (0:13-0:23)
- Visual: Admin opens Capture Command Center, pastes meeting notes, AI extracts commitments.
- Text: "Capture -> Extract -> Review -> Commit"
- VO: "Paste meeting notes, email threads, or chat history. Route5 extracts real commitments in seconds."

#### Scene 4 — Precision Assignment (0:23-0:33)
- Visual: Assign Task with full brief fields (context, directions, success criteria, effort, deadline, priority).
- Text: "Not reminders. Real delegation."
- VO: "Need direct control? Assign complete execution briefs, not vague reminders."

#### Scene 5 — Employee Activation (0:33-0:42)
- Visual: Employee gets in-app + email alert, opens My Inbox, starts task.
- Text: "In-app + Email Alerts"
- VO: "Assignees get notified instantly, with everything needed to execute."

#### Scene 6 — Live Execution (0:42-0:52)
- Visual: Split-screen employee progress + manager Desk/Org Feed update.
- Text: "Live status. No blind spots."
- VO: "As work moves, leaders see what is on track, at risk, and overdue, without chasing updates."

#### Scene 7 — Role Control (0:52-0:58)
- Visual: Organization Control shows role-based visibility settings.
- Text: "Role-aware control"
- VO: "Role-aware workspaces keep every team member focused on what matters."

#### Scene 8 — End Card (0:58-1:00)
- Visual: Product hero montage + logo.
- Text:
  - "Route5"
  - "Signal -> Structure -> Assign -> Execute"
  - "Command the work."
- VO: "Route5. Command the work."
